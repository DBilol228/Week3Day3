# Week3Day3
URelocation 🌍💼One platform meeting all your relocation needs.  URelocation is an all-in-one digital ecosystem designed to streamline, automate, and simplify the entire global mobility and relocation lifecycle. Whether you are an HR professional managing corporate transfers, an expat moving for a new job, or a digital nomad shifting bases, URelocation consolidates logistics, immigration, housing, and integration into a single seamless interface. 
Key Features

Customized Relocation Checklists: Dynamic, step-by-step timelines generated automatically based on your destination, origin, and family profile.

Immigration & Visa Automation: Integrated tracking for visas, work permits, and residency documentation with automated deadline alerts.

Housing & Neighborhood Discovery: Virtual tours, verified local real estate listings, and AI-driven neighborhood matching tailored to your budget and lifestyle. 

Vetted Vendor Marketplace: Direct access to trusted, pre-negotiated service providers including moving companies, pet relocation services, tax advisors, and language tutors.

Expense & Budget Management: Comprehensive dashboard to track all relocation expenditures, receipts, and corporate reimbursement pipelines.

Settling-In Assistance: Local community guides, banking and utility setup wizards, and neighborhood forums to ease cultural integration.  

Architecture & Tech Stack

URelocation is built on a scalable, cloud-native microservices architecture designed for high availability and strict data compliance.  Frontend: React TailwindCSS, TypeScript  
Backend: Node.js & Python (FastAPI for data-matching models)
